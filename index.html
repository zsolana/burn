The Problem

Our Solution

Revenue Model

Market Strategy

We monitor emerging meme trends across social platforms, acquiring IP rights proactively before viral explosion. Our legal team enforces rights across all major memecoin platforms, creating systematic coverage of the entire ecosystem.

The Flywheel Effect

Each successful enforcement generates fees that fund more IP acquisitions. More IP holdings increase our market coverage. Greater coverage generates more fees. This self-reinforcing cycle creates inevitable market dominance in the memecoin IP space.

Legal Framework

Our acquisitions focus on trademark registrations, copyright assignments, and domain ownership. These established IP protections give us clear legal standing to enforce rights against unauthorized commercial use by cryptocurrency platforms.
